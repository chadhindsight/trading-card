Cheetah

Black stripes run from their eyes down to the corners of their mouths.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx Jubatus
  • Average Length: 65-80 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: Savannas

Built more like greyhounds than typical cats, cheetahs are adapted for brief but intense bursts of speed. They have wiry bodies and small heads. Their coats are golden or yellowish, embellished with many small black spots, and their tails are long with a few black bands and sometimes a white tip.
